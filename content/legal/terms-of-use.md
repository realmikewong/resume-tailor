## What Taylor Resumé Does

Taylor Resumé is a web-based tool that helps you tailor your resume to a specific job description. You provide your resume and a job description. We send both, along with a prompt we write, to an AI language model (Claude, made by Anthropic). The AI returns a revised version of your resume shaped to the job you are targeting.
